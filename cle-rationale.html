Rationale


   Rationale
                        

    Our aim by promoting this advocacy is to foster the initiative to prevent the escalation of suicidal acts. It will grant the citizens the knowledge they require to refrain from the temptation to take oneself's life. This presented espousal will support the people's actions and insights towards the very sensitive topic of suicide. It will enlighten them that every problem has a solution and that killing one's self is a waste of human sanity. These solutions could shift the perspectives of those who are suffering from depression, those who are experiencing a lot of adversities, and those who have nothing left in their life, into a more positive and lively point of view. These aspirations could give people hope, a chance to live a new life. We're all God's children, therefore we must love, respect, and care for one another. We are all equally favored by Him therefore we must continually exude optimism throughout our lives.

 		                        
   Solutions

- Suicide is not a certain conclusion for anyone. We can prevent suicides and save lives by initiating the dialogue, giving support, and directing aid to those who need it.

                        
                        

- Evidence demonstrates that offering support services, talking about suicide, limiting access to self-harming devices, and following up with loved ones are all measures we can all take to aid others.

-By providing instant counseling to anybody who may need it, local crisis centers provide crucial assistance and link people to local agencies at critical moments.

However, if they are still alive, they are anxiously looking for a way to avoid death.
